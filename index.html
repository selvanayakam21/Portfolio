PROJECT

CHILD SAFETY DEVICE

Wearable device
This project is a concept for a smart wearable device for little children. The main advantages of this wearable are that it can be used with any cellphone and does not require a large smartphone or a highly tech-savvy individual to operate. The purpose of this device is to help parents locate their children with ease. There are many wearable devices in the market that help track the daily activities of children and also help find the children using the wifi and Bluetooth services present on the devices. However, wifi and Bluetooth appear to be an unreliable medium of communication channels between parent and child. 
The major goal of this project is to regulate and keep monitor of the water level in the apartments and restaurant without wireless connetion .This project includes high- and low-level monitoring, If the water level rises or falls, the machine will shut off automatically, and a buzzer will sound to let users notice.In contrast to other water level controllers, this project was carried out using wireless techniques, which helped to lower the environmental cost of the wires.I learned about some electric devices and how to use them to solve problems in real life through this project.
